Research Interests

  • I am interested in combining classical probabilistic models with modern deep learning techniques to create powerful machine learning frameworks that naturally incorporate quantifiable uncertainty estimates, human-interpretable model structures and suitable priors. I have primarily worked on these topics in the context of audiovisual signal processing, but I am also keen to explore new possibilities in other application areas like natural language processing.
